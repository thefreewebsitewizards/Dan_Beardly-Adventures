Adventure Stories

Born from a passion for adventure

Built for brands that go beyond the grid

Overlanding didn’t start as a branding opportunity or a business idea for me. It started as a way to reset. I’ve always been someone who adapts. Moved around a lot. Learned to figure things out, start over, keep going. What stuck through it all was the outdoors—hiking trails in New Mexico, chasing the horizon, finding peace in the quiet places.

At 17, I joined the Marines. I spent four years learning how to lead under pressure, think fast, and make things happen when the stakes were high. That mindset drives everything I do.

After the military, I went from retail leadership to vet consulting, and eventually landed in fintech sales.

By 2013, I was helping companies grow from the ground up—learning how to build brand strategy, drive revenue, and tell better stories. Most of the time with zero budget and a whole lot of hustle.

But in 2019, my life changed. I was 450 pounds and worn out. I committed to change—not just physically, but mentally. I got back outside. Started moving again. And somewhere between the trails and the open road, I reconnected with the version of myself I’d forgotten.

When COVID hit everything went remote—and I realized I didn’t need to stay put. I started traveling more. Camping. Exploring. I took a two-week trip to Utah and came back with a clearer mind and a new sense of purpose.

Overlanding wasn’t just a weekends to me anymore. It was a lifestyle. One that helped me heal, think bigger, and build something of my own. In 2022, after being laid off from the company I helped scale, I decided I wasn’t going back—I was building forward. That’s how GridLess Media started.
